World's Best Lasagna


What You'll Need

The Allrecipes community adores this lasagna recipe because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:

  • Meat: lean ground beef and sweet italian saugage
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, two cans of tomato paste
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Spices and seasonings: fresh parsley, dried basil, italian seasonings, black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta
  • One egg

Instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna: Meat sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, meat sauce, parmesan, then repeat.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 50 minutes at 375 F.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
